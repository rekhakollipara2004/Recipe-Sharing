ROLLS AND SHEWARMAS

ROLLS

Chicken Roll

To make Chicken Rolls, marinate chicken with spices, yogurt, and lemon juice. Grill or cook the marinated chicken until done. Place it in a flatbread or roti, add onions, chutney, and sauces of choice, and roll it tightly. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.

Veg Roll

For Veg Rolls, sauté a mixture of julienned vegetables like carrots, capsicum, and cabbage in oil with spices and salt. Place the cooked vegetables on a flatbread or roti, spread chutney or sauce, and roll it tightly. Serve warm with a dip of your choice.

Egg Roll

To make Egg Rolls, cook a beaten egg on a flatbread or roti. Add sliced onions, green chilies, and sauces over the cooked egg. Roll it up and serve hot with ketchup or green chutney.

SHAWARMA

Chicken Shawarma

To make Chicken Shawarma, marinate chicken with a blend of Middle Eastern spices, yogurt, and lemon juice. Grill or roast the chicken until tender. Place the cooked chicken on a flatbread or pita, add garlic sauce, pickles, and salad, and roll it. Serve warm with extra sauce.

Veg Shawarma

For Veg Shawarma, marinate and grill a mix of paneer or tofu with spices and yogurt. Fill a flatbread or pita with the cooked paneer, garlic sauce, pickles, and fresh salad. Roll it up and serve warm with tahini or garlic dip.

Egg Shawarma

To make Egg Shawarma, scramble eggs with spices and cook until done. Place the eggs on a flatbread or pita, add garlic sauce, onions, and pickles, then roll it up. Serve hot with a side of spicy sauce or salad.
