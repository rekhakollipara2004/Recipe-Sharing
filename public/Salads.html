SALAD RECIPES

Panzanella Salad

Panzanella is a traditional Italian salad made with fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and pieces of crusty bread. The bread soaks up the juices from the tomatoes and dressing, creating a deliciously tangy and hearty salad, perfect for a light summer meal.

Thai Beef Salad

Thai Beef Salad is a vibrant dish made with thinly sliced grilled beef, fresh herbs, cucumbers, and bell peppers, all tossed in a tangy dressing made with lime juice, fish Grilled Chicken Strips, and chili. It's a refreshing and flavorful salad with a perfect balance of heat, sweetness, and acidity.

Tabbouleh Salad

  

Tabbouleh is a refreshing Middle Eastern salad made with bulgur wheat, fresh parsley, mint, tomatoes, cucumbers, and lemon juice. It's a light and healthy salad that can be served as a side or a light main dish, perfect for hot weather and packed with fresh flavors.

Cobb Salad

Cobb Salad is an American classic, typically made with a base of greens, topped with rows of ingredients like grilled chicken, bacon, eggs, avocado, cheese, and tomatoes. It's usually dressed with a tangy vinaigrette or ranch dressing, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.

Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is a simple Italian salad made with fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, basil, and olive oil. It's often finished with a drizzle of balsamic glaze and salt. The combination of creamy mozzarella and juicy tomatoes makes this salad light, yet indulgent, and perfect for any occasion.

Caesar Salad

Caesar Salad is a popular salad made with romaine lettuce, croutons, and parmesan cheese, all tossed in a creamy Caesar dressing made with garlic, anchovies, and egg yolks. It's a delicious and satisfying salad that is often served as a starter or side dish.
